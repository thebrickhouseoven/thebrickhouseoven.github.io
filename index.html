Our Restaurant

The BrickHouse Oven opened its doors for the first time on November 21st 2017. Our intention was to provide the People of Trivandrum with what we felt was lacking at the time, stunning Ambience, excellent coffee, friendly service, value for money and quality fresh food. We wanted Voyage to be a home away from home for all of our customers to enjoy and feel welcome.
